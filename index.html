About me

I am a Master's student in Mathematics at the Instituto de Matemáticas, Unidad Cuernavaca, UNAM working under the supervision of Agustín Romano. I previously obtained my Bachelor's degree from the Facultad de Ciencias Físico Matemáticas, BUAP in 2024.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in algebraic geometry and commutative algebra, with a particular focus on singularity theory.

Currently, I am focusing on generalizing the relationship between the minimal resolution and the higher Nash modification of affine varieties.
